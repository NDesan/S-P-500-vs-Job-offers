# S&P 500 vs. Job Offers: The AI Impact

**Analyzing the Decoupling of Corporate Performance and Employment Trends**

---

## 📌 **Overview**
This project explores the evolving relationship between the **S&P 500** (a benchmark for the performance of large U.S. companies) and the **job market**, with a focus on how **AI and automation** are reshaping traditional correlations.

Historically, economic growth and job creation moved in tandem. However, as AI and automation advance, companies are achieving record profits and productivity **without proportional increases in hiring**. This repository visualizes and analyzes this **decoupling effect**, highlighting its implications for the future of work.

---

## 🎯 **Key Questions**
- How has the correlation between S&P 500 performance and job offers changed over time?
- What role does AI play in this shift?
- What are the long-term implications for workers, businesses, and policymakers?

---

- **S&P 500 Data**: Historical stock market performance (e.g., from Yahoo Finance, Bloomberg, or FRED).
